I like the hands on curriculum.. It was very easy to follow and it was very easy to get the app going. It was great to meet everyone and everyone was so friendly & enthusiastic. I enjoyed the fact that the activities were mostly self-paced. I was most taken aback by the willingness of everyone to help one another.
